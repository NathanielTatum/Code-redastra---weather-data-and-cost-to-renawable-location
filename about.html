Motivation & Problem Statement

Optimizing renewable energy placement is crucial for maximizing energy output, reducing costs, and minimizing environmental impact. Our AI-driven approach helps identify the best locations for solar and wind projects by analyzing weather, geography, and economics.

How It Works

We take the weather data from large national databases and then compare that to known statitistics on solar and wind energy production which we then use to find locally optimal positioning for maximum benefit and minimizing costs using our sophisticated AI known as Aneious

Project Summary

  • Analyze large-scale weather & spatial data
  • Predict solar & wind energy yield
  • Optimize for weather, cost, and location
  • Simulate scenarios for maximizing ROI or minimizing impact

Data Sources

  • GHCN‑Daily: Daily temperature, precipitation, snowfall, etc.
  • ISD: Hourly and synoptic global weather observations
  • GHCN on AWS
  • ISD on AWS

AI Techniques

  • Regression Models: Predict energy output
  • Clustering: Group high-potential zones
  • Neural Networks: Model complex relationships
  • Time Series Forecasting: Predict future energy availability
  • Geospatial Analysis + AI: Combine spatial data with ML

Key Data Types

  • Weather data (wind speed, solar irradiance,cloud coverage)
  • Geographic data (lat, long, elevation)
  • Land cost data
  • Grid infrastructure locations
  • Zoning/environmental data
