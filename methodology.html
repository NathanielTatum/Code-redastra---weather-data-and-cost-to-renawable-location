How It Works

Discover the AI and data pipeline powering optimal renewable energy placement.

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Data Collection: Using a specified longitude and latitude value, Aneious collects relevant weather info from NASA's climate data API.
  2. Preprocessing: We then combine this raw data with user inputs to prevent temperature or wind speed from causing too much variance outside of the cell’s operational limits.
  3. Gemini AI Studio Model: The processed data is then passed to our AI model, which analyzes patterns, interprets energy potential, and communicates results through the chat interface.
  4. Cesium Ion Interactive Map: This same data is simultaneously sent to a Cesium Ion interactive map, allowing the user to visually compare AI-selected sites and verify accuracy.
  5. User Conclusion: Given this data, the user can experiment with variables until they find the most efficient and cost-effective location for renewable development.

System Architecture

We use a chatbot powered by Gemini AI for natural language interaction, a Cesium Ion library for 3D visualization, and NASA’s open data resources for accurate environmental inputs.

Techniques Used

  • Data Collection and Preprocessing: The AI gathers weather and environmental data from NASA’s API, cleans and normalizes it, and combines it with user inputs to ensure consistency and reliability.
  • Machine Learning and Predictive Modeling: Gemini AI applies trained regression and neural network models to identify optimal renewable energy placement by correlating environmental variables such as solar radiation, wind speed, and cost efficiency.
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP): The chatbot interprets user queries using tokenization, intent recognition, and contextual analysis, allowing natural interaction and personalized recommendations.
  • Optimization and Decision Logic: The system performs multi-variable optimization to recommend the best renewable energy sites, balancing cost, output potential, and environmental impact through weighted scoring and decision algorithms.
  • Continuous Learning and Adaptation: Aneious continuously refines its models through feedback and new data inputs, improving accuracy and adaptability to changing environmental and cost conditions.
