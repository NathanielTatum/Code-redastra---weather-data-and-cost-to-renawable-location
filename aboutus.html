About Code Redastra

Weather data, renewable location modeling and cost estimates — explained. Learn who we are and how we build reliable insights.

Our Mission

We provide accurate, open data and tools that help communities and planners evaluate renewable energy potential and costs using local weather patterns and infrastructure data. We emphasize transparency, reproducibility, and actionable insight.

What we do

Collect and normalize weather datasets, model renewable resource yields, and estimate installation and lifecycle costs. We present results through clear visuals and downloadable data so decision-makers can act.

Datasets: satellites, ground stations, derived models.

Team

Small multi-disciplinary team of meteorologists, data scientists, and engineers focused on practical tooling for energy planning. We collaborate with local partners to validate models on the ground.

History

Started as a research project to compare solar yield estimates across regions, the project matured into a platform that combines open weather archives with cost models to produce localized forecasts and cost breakdowns.
